Project Overview
This repository contains my final project for the CSE0001 - Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have developed this portfolio to demonstrate essential digital skills, professional online presence, and cybersecurity awareness for my peers.

Module Summaries

Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Goal: Create a visual resource for students explaining digital literacy.
Outcome: A one-page infographic designed in Canva covering essential tools and safe internet practices.

Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
Goal: Establish a professional presence on key academic and industry platforms.
Outcome: Profiles set up on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle, including a personalized GitHub Profile README.

Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Goal: Demonstrate proficiency in technical problem-solving and cloud tools.
Outcome: Completed a HackerRank coding challenge and developed a digital literacy quiz.
Google Form Link: https://forms.gle/dUxXHwpMEekCFmT1A

Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
Goal: Master professional digital communication.
Outcome: Drafted formal emails for academic and internship inquiries, and created a social media "Do's and Don'ts" guide.

Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
Goal: Research and educate others on online threats.
Outcome: Conducted a case study on UPI/Online Payment Fraud and developed a safety checklist featuring the 1930 Helpline.
